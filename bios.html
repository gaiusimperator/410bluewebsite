Greg Raver-Lampman


Role: Mentor

Greg Raver-Lampman is an instructor at the Old Dominion University English Language Center. While he recently began intensive English teaching in the U.S., he has taught around the world, starting in his 20s when he taught advanced English to students aspiring to study in the U.S. In addition to teaching English, he has published two books, written for newspapers and magazines and also written for television, including the FBI Files and Discovery Health. He received his masters in Applied Linguistics at ODU and researches English language teaching and phonology.
